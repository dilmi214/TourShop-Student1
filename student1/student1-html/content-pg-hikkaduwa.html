Introduction

Since its discovery in the 1960s, Hikkaduwa's beaches have been a popular attraction for tourists.

The beach extends for several kilometers and offers a variety of entertaining activities for visitors to enjoy.

Those seeking adventure can engage in water sports such as snorkeling and scuba diving. The clear waters of the beach are also appealing to nature enthusiasts who can take a swim and witness the colorful aquatic life.

The coral sanctuary is home to stunning coral formations, as well as a variety of fish and large turtles.

The islets along the beach provide habitats for pockets of wildlife and offer an opportunity for visitors to interact with them.

Hikkaduwa is renowned for its delectable seafood curries, soothing massages, Buddhist temples, and a range of water activities such as surfing, snorkeling, and diving.

Coral Reefs & Glass Bottom Boat Ride

Exploring the underwater world by taking a dip in the water is one of the most enjoyable activities to experience at Hikkaduwa Beach

The beach boasts magnificent coral reefs, with one of the two marine national parks in Sri Lanka encompassing a fringing coral reef that reaches a depth of approximately five meters.

The national park is situated near the northern end of the shoreline and is an ideal destination to witness the submerged beauty of Sri Lanka. Snorkeling gear can be rented from nearby shops.

The best time to visit the coral reefs is from November to April when the calmness of the water offers excellent visibility.

One of the top attractions in Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka, is taking a trip on a glass-bottom boat to explore the sea.

These boats are equipped with glass or transparent bottoms, providing tourists with a unique opportunity to witness the underwater world in the most straightforward way possible.

You can catch a glimpse of the vibrant beauty of the underwater world without getting wet!

Tea Zone

This location is ideal for tea lovers and those who are interested in trying out different varieties of teas. The tea-tasting experience allows you to sample various types of teas that you may have never tried before and then select your preferred option to purchase.

The range of teas available in Hikkaduwa's tea zones is impressive, with unique and distinct varieties that tantalize your taste buds. The list includes coconut tea, Kandy tea, mixed fruits tea, lime tea, soursop tea, black tea, and more.

The tea zone's atmosphere is inviting, with a pleasant aroma that fills the air, and you can see tea plantations surrounding the area.

Ayurvedic Treatments

If you're feeling tired after surfing or exploring the town, you can find solace in the many centers and shops that offer signature Sri Lankan ayurvedic treatments and massages. These treatments are designed to help you relax, rejuvenate and re-energize your body and mind.

From traditional ayurvedic massages to aromatherapy treatments and more, Hikkaduwa's ayurvedic centers offer a wide variety of treatments that cater to every need.

The friendly and professional staff at these centers and shops are committed to providing a holistic and rejuvenating experience for visitors to Hikkaduwa. With their expertise and personalized approach, they make sure that every visitor receives the best care and attention.

Whether you're looking for a relaxing massage or a complete ayurvedic treatment, Hikkaduwa's centers and shops have got you covered. So, take some time off from your busy schedule and indulge in some self-care.
